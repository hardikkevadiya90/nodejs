Fruits and Their Benefits

  • Apple
    • Rich in fiber and antioxidants
    • Supports heart health
  • Banana
    • Good source of potassium
    • Provides energy and supports digestion
  • Orange
    • High in vitamin C
    • Boosts the immune system
  • Strawberry
    • Rich in antioxidants
    • Supports skin health
  • Grapes
    • Contain resveratrol, which may have health benefits
    • Support heart health
  • Mango
    • High in vitamin A and vitamin C
    • Supports eye health
  • Pineapple
    • Rich in vitamin C and manganese
    • Supports digestion
  • Kiwi
    • Excellent source of vitamin C and vitamin K
    • Supports immune health
  • Blueberry
    • Rich in antioxidants
    • Supports brain health
  • Watermelon
    • High water content, hydrating fruit
    • Refreshing and low in calories
