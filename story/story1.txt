Vikram and Vetal, also known as Vikram and Betal, is a famous collection of folktales from India. The stories revolve around King Vikramaditya and the wise vampire-like being known as Vetal. Here's a brief summary of one of the tales:

Title: The Story of King Vikramaditya and Vetal

Once, King Vikramaditya was challenged by a sage to capture a Vetal, a supernatural being, who could possess dead bodies. To achieve this, King Vikramaditya visited a haunted forest where he found a hanging corpse. As he attempted to capture the Vetal, the being started telling him stories.
